---
title: Not a Valentine
note: Probably I won't send this one.
---

There is a thing I have not said to you, and the reason I have not said it is that I think you already know, and saying it would feel like reaching for the light switch in a room that's already lit.

But just in case.

I think you are the most attentive person I have ever met. You notice the temperature of a room as soon as you enter it. You notice when I have been pretending to listen to a podcast and you notice when I am actually listening to one. You notice the way the woman at the corner shop is having a bad week, and you remember her name, and you ask after the dog.

I used to think attention was a small thing. It is not a small thing. It might be the largest thing.

If you are reading this, then I gave it to you after all, which means I was braver than I thought I would be, and you should know that you are the reason for that.

Not a valentine.

— m.
