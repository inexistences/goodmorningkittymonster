---
title: The Thing About Tuesdays
note: Recorded after the fact — close to verbatim.
---

I told you, sitting on the kitchen floor, that I have always been a little afraid of Tuesdays. Not afraid like a phobia. Afraid like Tuesdays are the day where you find out whether the week is going to be a good week or a hard one.

You laughed and said that was the most unhinged thing you'd heard all month, and then a minute later you said: *but yes, actually, that's true, isn't it.*

That's a thing I love about you. The arriving at agreement only after the laughing.

I think I also said — and you can correct me on this — that I'd rather have a hard Tuesday with you than an easy one without. Which sounds like the kind of thing people put on a mug. But I meant it then and I mean it now, and I will keep meaning it on the harder Tuesdays especially.
